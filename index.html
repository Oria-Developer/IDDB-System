No Database?

IDDB (Internal Discord Database) provides a streamlined approach to data persistence using Discord channels as storage infrastructure.

About IDDB System

The Problem

  • Traditional databases require complex setup and maintenance
  • Hosting costs add up quickly for bot developers
  • Data persistence becomes a bottleneck in bot performance
  • Complex backup and recovery systems are needed

Our Solution

  • Leverage Discord's existing infrastructure as your database
  • Zero additional hosting costs - use what you already pay for
  • Background processing ensures your bot stays responsive
  • Built-in resilience with automatic recovery after restarts

Why Choose IDDB System?

We're bot developers who understand the pain points of data persistence. IDDB System was born from real-world experience managing large-scale Discord bots where traditional database solutions proved costly and complex.

System Advantages

Zero Resource Impact

By offloading writes to a background worker, your bot's interaction response times remain lightning-fast.

Crash Resilient

Because every log is uniquely ID'd, you can cross-reference logs after a restart to ensure no data gaps exist.

Channel-as-a-DB

The target channel becomes your table. Messages are your rows. JSON content is your column data.
